About Me

Hi there, I'm Sukyoung!

I am a NYC-based Product Designer with a strong interest in Experience Design and Interactive Media. Currently, I am a junior pursuing a BFA in Design & Technology at Parsons School of Design, with a minor in Psychology. My work experience spans across roles at NJ TRANSIT, The New School, Neuraxis, and various start-ups, where I've contributed to creating both digital and physical consumer experiences.

In my free time, I immerse myself at inspiring galleries and museums, painting my thoughts on canvas, and long walks around my city :)
